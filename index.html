About

Hello, world!
I'm Jeannie. I love solving problems, and I've always had an innate need to be creative. My career in development has provided me the perfect way to utilize both my creative designs and logical skills to solve real-world human problems.


I have over 11 years of experience in research & visual communications, as well as experience in both frontend and fullstack development. I love working on focused, goal-oriented, and collaborative teams.


I'm highly-adaptable and thrive in fast-paced, deadline-driven environments, and am always looking for opportunities to expand my portfolio.
